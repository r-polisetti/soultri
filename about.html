Our Journey

Our Story, Baked with Love

From a small home kitchen to a growing bakery brand, Soultri was built on passion, patience, and the joy of creating desserts that bring people together.

How It All Started

Every bakery has a beginning. Ours started with simple recipes, endless experimentation, and a dream to bring handmade baked goods to our community.

What began as baking for friends and family slowly turned into something much bigger — a place where every cake, pastry, and loaf carries a piece of our story.

Today, Soultri continues to blend traditional baking techniques with modern creativity to craft unforgettable experiences.

Our Journey

A timeline of milestones that shaped Soultri into what it is today.

2015 — Humble Beginnings

We started baking from home, sharing cakes and fresh bread with friends and neighbors.

2017 — Growing Demand

Orders began increasing through word of mouth, turning our passion into a growing business.

2020 — First Bakery Store

We opened our first bakery store to serve our community with fresh baked creations every day.

Today — Baking Happiness Daily

Soultri continues to innovate while staying true to its roots: fresh, honest, handcrafted baking.
